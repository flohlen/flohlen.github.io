About me

My name is Florian and I am a 18 year old from Kassel. I am currently studying Computer Science and Civil Engineering at the University of Kassel. I have experience in building autonomous robots and being a Lifeguard and Nursing/Care Assistant.

Projects

ODLC - OmniDirectional LaserCutter

Developing and building an autonomous omnidirectional lasercutter for the home market. The robot utilizes omnidirectional wheels and optical triangulation. It is driven by stepper motors and an custom programmed arduino. Currently on hold.

Lifeguard

Upkeeping of the swimming pool by monitoring the technical facilities as well as the chemical distribution inside the pool. Entertaining and keeping the guests safe. This includes a variety of different activities for kids and adults.

Nursing/Care Assistant

Caring for seniors suffering from dementia and various limitations. Day to day entertainment by playing games, having different social interactions and self-developed and directed entertainment programs. Prepairing dinner and other meals together with residents in a group setting.
